Departments We have

Computer Engineering

Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) is a dynamic field at the intersection of technology and innovation. It explores algorithms, software development, hardware design, AI, and more, driving the digital revolution and shaping our modern world..

Mechanical Engineering

Mechanical engineering combines physics and mathematics to design, analyze, and manufacture machinery and systems. Mechanical engineers innovate in areas like robotics, HVAC, automotive, and aerospace, ensuring efficient and reliable mechanical systems for various industries.


Electronics Engineering

Electronics engineering is a field focused on designing, developing, and maintaining electronic systems and devices. It encompasses areas such as circuit design, microelectronics, digital signal processing, and telecommunications, playing a vital role in advancing technology and innovation across various industries..


Electrical Engineering Engineering

Electrical engineering is a diverse field that encompasses the study and application of electricity, electronics, and electromagnetism. It involves designing, analyzing, and maintaining electrical systems, from power generation and distribution to electronic devices and communication systems. Electrical engineers play a crucial role in modern technology and infrastructure..
